About Me

My name is Maryam, and I am the creator behind رائحة kamshi by maryam. I have a deep love for fragrance and magic of scents especially the traditional art of Turaren Wuta.

I also have a strong passion for technology. While my journey hasn't always been linear, I've stayed curious and committed to learning, especially web development. My goal is to blend tech and tradition in a beautiful, meaningful ways.

I believe that scents can calm the mind and inspire the soul! and I also believe that technology can help bring that experience to people everywhere.
